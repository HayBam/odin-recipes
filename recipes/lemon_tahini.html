Lemon Tahini Dressing

This tangy, classic dressing with a hint of sesame is ready in 5 minutes. Not only tasty on a salad, but drizzle it over roasted or grilled veggies, just about any side dish.

Ingredients

  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 3 tablespoons tahini paste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 clove garlic, roughly chopped
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

How to make Lemon Tahini
  1. Step 1: Combine lemon juice, tahini, olive oil, water, garlic, salt, and pepper in a mini food processor or blender; process until pureed and smooth.
  2. Step 2: f dressing is too thick, add 1 teaspoon water and blend to thin. Continue adding water by the teaspoon until you reach your desired consistency.
